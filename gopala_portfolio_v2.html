About Me

I'm Gopala Krishna — a Creative Producer and Director based in New York City, co-founder of Evolve Pictures LLC. I own projects end-to-end: from the first story conversation all the way to final export and publishing.

My short film "Boundaries" — written, produced, directed, and edited entirely solo — was an Official Selection at Lighthouse International Film Festival 2025. I hold a Master's in Media Studies and bring 3+ years of hands-on experience across film, branded content, and digital video.

What I find most rewarding about production is the human orchestration of it — connecting a cinematographer, editor, sound designer, and talent toward one shared vision, and building the conditions where all of it clicks together. I show up fully and take real ownership of the outcome, not just the task.

Beyond film, I shoot photography (travel, portraits, sports, design), write across formats (narrative scripts, ad copy, academic papers), and manage the full production binder for every project I run.
